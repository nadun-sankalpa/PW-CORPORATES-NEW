Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accounting services

What accounting services do you offer for small businesses?

We offer a comprehensive range of accounting services for small businesses, including bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, tax planning and filing, payroll processing, and financial analysis. Our services are scalable and can be tailored to meet the specific needs of your business.

How can your accounting services help my business save money?

Our accounting services can help your business save money in several ways. First, we ensure tax compliance while identifying legitimate tax deductions and credits. Second, we provide financial insights that help you identify cost-saving opportunities. Third, we help prevent costly errors and penalties. Finally, outsourcing your accounting to us is often more cost-effective than hiring in-house staff.

Do you work with businesses in specific industries?

Yes, we have experience working with businesses across various industries in Sri Lanka, including retail, hospitality, manufacturing, technology, and professional services. Our team understands the unique accounting requirements and challenges of different sectors, allowing us to provide industry-specific solutions.

How often will I receive financial reports?

The frequency of financial reports depends on your service package and business needs. Typically, we provide monthly financial statements, but we can also arrange for quarterly or weekly reporting. We work with you to establish a reporting schedule that gives you the financial visibility you need to make informed business decisions.

What accounting software do you use?

We are proficient in various accounting software platforms, including QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, and local Sri Lankan accounting systems. We can work with your existing software or recommend and implement a solution that best fits your business requirements and budget.
