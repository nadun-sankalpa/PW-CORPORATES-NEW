Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our tourism services

What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka has a tropical climate with distinct dry and wet seasons that vary by region. The best time to visit depends on which areas you plan to explore. Generally, December to March is ideal for the west and south coasts and the hill country, while April to September is better for the east coast. The Cultural Triangle in the north-central region can be visited year-round. We can help you plan your trip based on your preferred travel dates and destinations.

Do I need a visa to visit Sri Lanka?

Yes, most visitors to Sri Lanka require a visa, which can be obtained through the Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) system online before arrival. The ETA allows for a 30-day stay and can be extended for up to 6 months after arrival. Some countries have visa exemption agreements with Sri Lanka. As part of our service, we can provide guidance on the visa application process and requirements based on your nationality.

What types of accommodation do you offer?

We offer a wide range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets. These include luxury resorts, boutique hotels, eco-friendly lodges, heritage properties, beach villas, and authentic homestays. All accommodations we recommend meet our quality standards for cleanliness, comfort, service, and location. We can customize your accommodation choices based on your preferences and budget requirements.

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking your tour at least 3-6 months in advance, especially if you plan to travel during the peak season (December to March) or if you have specific accommodation preferences. This allows us sufficient time to secure the best accommodations and experiences for your trip. However, we can also arrange last-minute tours based on availability. For special events or holidays like Christmas or New Year, booking 6-12 months in advance is advisable.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. For tour packages, we typically require a 25% deposit at the time of booking to secure your reservation, with the remaining balance due 30 days before your arrival. For custom itineraries or group bookings, payment terms may vary. All payment details will be clearly outlined in your tour proposal.
