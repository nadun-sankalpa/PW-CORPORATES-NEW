Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our property management services

What services are included in your property management package?

Our comprehensive property management package includes tenant screening and placement, rent collection, maintenance coordination, financial reporting, legal compliance, property inspections, and 24/7 emergency response. We also offer marketing services to minimize vacancies and strategic planning to maximize your property's value. We can customize our services based on your specific needs and property type.

How much do your property management services cost?

Our property management fees typically range from 8% to 12% of the monthly rental income, depending on the property type, size, and the specific services required. We also charge a tenant placement fee equal to 50% of one month's rent when we find a new tenant. For vacation rentals, our fee structure is different and based on a percentage of the booking revenue. We're transparent about our fees and would be happy to provide a detailed quote based on your specific property.

How do you screen potential tenants?

We conduct a thorough screening process that includes credit checks, employment verification, income verification, previous landlord references, and background checks. We verify that potential tenants have a stable income (typically 3 times the monthly rent), a positive rental history, and no relevant criminal history. Our rigorous screening process helps ensure that we place reliable, responsible tenants who will take good care of your property and pay rent on time.

How do you handle maintenance and repairs?

We have a network of licensed, insured, and pre-vetted contractors and maintenance professionals who provide quality work at reasonable rates. For minor repairs (typically under Rs. 25,000), we handle them immediately and include them in your monthly statement. For major repairs, we contact you with estimates for approval before proceeding. We also conduct regular preventive maintenance to catch small issues before they become expensive problems. Our 24/7 emergency response system ensures that urgent issues are addressed promptly.

How often will I receive updates about my property?

We provide monthly financial statements detailing all income and expenses related to your property. These statements are available through our secure online portal, which you can access at any time. We also conduct quarterly property inspections and send you detailed reports with photos. Additionally, we'll contact you immediately regarding any significant issues or decisions that require your input. Our property managers are always available to discuss your property and answer any questions you may have.
