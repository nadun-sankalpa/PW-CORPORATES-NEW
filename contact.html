Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does PW Corporates offer?

PW Corporates offers a comprehensive range of business services including Accounting, Property Management, Show Money services, Salary Management, Legal Advice, and Tourism services. Each service is designed to help businesses and individuals navigate their respective areas with professional expertise and support.

How can I schedule a consultation?

Scheduling a consultation with PW Corporates is easy. You can fill out the contact form on this page, call us directly at +94 11 234 5678, or email us at info@pwcorporates.lk. Our team will respond promptly to arrange a consultation at a time that works for you. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations to accommodate your preferences and schedule.

What are your service fees?

Our service fees vary depending on the specific service and the scope of work required. We believe in transparent pricing and will provide a detailed quote after understanding your specific needs during the initial consultation. We offer competitive rates and flexible payment options to accommodate different budgets and requirements. For some services, we offer package deals and retainer options for ongoing support.

Do you serve clients outside of Colombo?

Yes, we serve clients throughout Sri Lanka and even international clients with interests in Sri Lanka. While our main office is located in Colombo, we offer virtual consultations and services for clients who cannot visit us in person. Our team is experienced in working with remote clients and ensuring seamless communication and service delivery regardless of location.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. Our team is committed to providing prompt and attentive service to all clients and potential clients. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at +94 11 234 5678 during business hours. We also have a dedicated support team that monitors our email outside of regular business hours for time-sensitive requests.
